(F)requently (A)sked (Q)uestions

Helping people help themselves

Here are some of the most asked questions. In case you can't find an answer please contact us via our contact form.


Personal Data

We neither transmit nor hand down your personal data to any third party. The only exception is the registration process. For the authentification we pass on your name, address, tax ID, date of birth and nationality. Your phone number and mail are for communication between us and you only.

How to terminate an account?

We can terminate your account 3 months after the arrival of ur written termination. Please use our contact form and our admins wil terminate it as wished. Please be aware, we do not delete your data for legal reasons. Also remember to withdraw all your money from your account, otherwise we will not be able to proceed.

How to do a withdrawal?

In your account management press the button "do withdrawal". You then can enter a specified amount you want to withdraw. We generate a PDF for you with a QR-Code, which you can use at any shop to get payed out there.
